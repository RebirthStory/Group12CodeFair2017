The IT Code Fair is on the 11th of November 2017 at Charles Darwin University!

What's on?

There are six major activities to look forward to:

Coding Competition

This competition is open to all IT students (VET, and Higher Education) who can create Apps, Programs, Mobile Prototypes, Websites, Games that demonstrate their developing knowledge, skills in building elegant, maintainable code that works!

Poster Competition

The poster competition aims to encourage students to communicate their IT work and develop and expand their visual communication skills.We encourage students whose IT pathways to a future career is in the more analytical aspects of the profession.

Code Hour Workshops

Supporting the global ‘Hour of Code’ initiative to develop coding literacies. Any high school teachers and students that are interested please join us!

Industry Employer Speed Dating

Employer Speed Dating provides students an opportunity to meet local IT industry employers. There are two speed dating categories for students to enter: Developer and IT Support.

Business Innovation Challenge

This challenge, provided by the NT government, presents a real-life problem to students regarding how to communicate NT Road conditions using 21st century technologies.

Academic Excellence Awards

This event is held to encourage a greater emphasis on academic endeavours and to recognise students who achieve highly in each subject taught in IT. Students who receive this award earn the highest scores in each unit.
