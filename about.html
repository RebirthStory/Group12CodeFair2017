IT Code Fair 2016

In 2016, the third IT Code Fair was held at Charles Darwin University (CDU) North Australian Centre for Oil and Gas. Attendees included a record number of students, industry partners and members of the IT and University communities.

Competition Winners

Winners of IT Code Fair 2016:

  • Business Innovation Challenge: Robert Jackson
  • Poster Competition: Ayush Ghai
  • Novice Coder: Joel Benesha
  • Intermediate Coder (Individual): Aayush Sapkota
  • Intermediate Coder (Team): Poonam Patel Muhammad Shahab Iftikhar
  • Advanced Coder (Individual): Robert Jackson
  • Advanced Coder (Team): Samuel Walledge and Phillip Davis

Other Events

Industry Employer Speed Dating

18 students took up the opportunity. Four new employers joined the 2016 Speed Dating and the timing for the ‘speed dating’ includes 1 minute for the student to make a pitch to a prospective employer and 2 minutes for the employer to ‘interview’ the students.

Code Hour Workshops

4 x 1 hour workshops were run for 45 students from 3 local high schools (Casuarina Senior College, Darwin High School and Essington) and university staff.
